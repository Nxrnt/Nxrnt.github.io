About Me

Hi, I’m Peter - a game designer and video editor who’s into building mechanically satisfying systems and clean UX. I mostly work in Unity (C#), and I like turning messy ideas into playable prototypes fast.

I’m currently working on a few small games as portfolio pieces, focusing on core loops, progression, and player feel - the stuff that makes a game “click”. I enjoy designing systems, tuning mechanics, and iterating from feedback until it feels right.

When I’m not making games, I’m usually editing videos/devlogs, tinkering with tools, or diving into a new project idea.
