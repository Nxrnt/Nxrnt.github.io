About

Hey! I’m Peter, online as Nix. I’m a Unity game designer and developer who builds small, system-driven games in C#. I care about clean mechanics, clear feedback, and tools that make iteration fast, from movement and UI to progression, saving, and simple editor workflows. I ship playable builds on itch.io, keep projects organised with Git, and I like turning messy ideas into something you can pick up and play in a few minutes. Right now I’m focused on building a stronger portfolio with short, polished projects and sharing progress through devlogs and clips.
